About beads

The jewelry world is set for a shakeup in the coming decade. Tired of the trend for minimalist necklaces, bracelets and earrings that defined much of the 2010s, designers and fashionistas alike are starting to have more fun with color, size and styling. If you’re wondering how to keep on top of the latest jewelry fashions in the coming months and years, we’ve put together a quick guide to some of the biggest emerging trends.

1. Dangly earrings

Gone are the days when simple silver studs or round gold hoops were enough to complete any outfit. Now that we have entered the 2020s, it is time to start experimenting with dangly earrings full of ornate beads and gemstones. One of the best things about this style of earring is that they can brighten up even the most basic of outfits, adding a little cheer to your day. For inspiration, check out our range of gorgeous earrings.

2. A splash of color

2020 is all about embracing a joyful aesthetic, so bold and beautiful colors are well and truly in style. From deep reds to exuberant blues, try to match your jewelry to your outfits and try not to go overboard. Fortunately, our pieces come in a range of breathtaking colors, one of which is sure to suit your requirements.
