Made using traditional freehand beading techniques with strong synthetic thread.

Sophisticated enough for a day at the office or a night out yet casual enough for down time. A great alternative to metal jewelry.

3 ear hook options to meet your metal sensitivity needs:

  1. Nickel free - Our default industry standard and nickel free though not completely hypoallergenic
  2. Sterling silver - Works for many people with mild to moderate sensitivities
  3. Niobium - Contains no nickel alloys at all and will usually work for the most sensitive ears

* Disclaimer: I provide top quality materials to offer an alternative for allergy sufferers BUT cannot guarantee that you will not have a reaction. Please wear at your own discretion.
