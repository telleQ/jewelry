Beading is Passion

My name is Lisa, I'm 24. I live in Ukraine. I have a lot of hobbies and interests, such as: photography, travelling, languages, music, chess and my biggest passion - beads. I started making beaded bracelets since my childhood, but a couple of years ago I found out about this wonderful bead crochet technique, and I fell in love with it completely. Now I'm creating necklaces, bracelets, earrings, etc. with all the kinds of top quality beads: Czech beads Preciosa, Japanese beads Toho, Miyuki Round and Delica. I'm always full with new ideas and no day goes by without beading.
